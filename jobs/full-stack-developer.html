Full Stack Developer

Are you passionate about crafting cutting-edge web applications in the AI industry?

Join Fidelity AI is on the lookout for a Full-Stack Developer to become an integral part of our dynamic and innovative team. This role is an exciting opportunity to collaborate closely with our skilled engineers and designers in developing robust, high-performance applications that cater to our diverse clientele.


About Us:
At Join Fidelity AI, we're committed to pushing the boundaries of AI technology. Our team is dedicated to delivering groundbreaking solutions that transform businesses and industries. We thrive in an intellectually stimulating environment and are seeking a talented individual who shares our drive for innovation.

Key Responsibilities:
As a Full-Stack Developer, you will spearhead the development and refinement of both front-end and back-end aspects of our applications. Your role will involve translating company and client requirements into functional, visually appealing, and interactive applications that leverage the power of AI.

Essential Experience:

  • Proficiency in front-end frameworks like React (strongly preferred) and experience with Next.js
  • Expertise in TypeScript and handling streaming responses
  • Familiarity with OAuth providers


Desirable Experience:

  • Familiarity with Vercel deployments
  • Practical exposure to AWS services
  • Previous involvement in AI projects, particularly in chatbots
  • Experience managing large search-based applications
  • Familiarity with Tailwind CSS

If you're passionate about pushing boundaries and thrive in a collaborative environment, apply today to contribute your expertise and help drive innovation forward.
